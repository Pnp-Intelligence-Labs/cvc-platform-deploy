# Team Invite Templates

Copy, edit, and send when you're ready to bring the team onto the platform.
Two versions: one for Slack/Teams, one for email.

---

## Slack / Teams Message

```
Hey team — we've set up our new internal ops platform. It's live and ready to use.

🔗 URL: [your platform URL]

Your login:
  Username: [their username]
  Temp password: [their temp password]

Log in and change your password right away (top-right menu → Change Password).

The platform covers:
• Deal flow and company database (Ventures)
• Partner CRM (Partners)
• Sales pipeline, requests, and meeting notes

A quick user guide is here: [link to USER_GUIDE.md or paste the URL]

Let me know if you run into anything.
```

---

## Email Version

**Subject:** Your login for [Team Name] Ops Platform

```
Hi [Name],

We've launched our internal operations platform — it replaces the spreadsheets
we've been using for deal flow and partner tracking.

Access it here: [your platform URL]

Your login:
  Username:  [their username]
  Password:  [temporary password — they'll set their own on first login]

First thing: log in and change your password (click your name in the top-right
corner → Change Password).

What's in the platform:
- Ventures — our full company database with deal flow stages, enrichment data,
  and a searchable pipeline
- Partners — our corporate partner CRM with match tracking and notes
- Sales Pipeline — outbound partner development
- Requests — inbound partnership requests
- Meeting Notes — structured notes tied to companies and partners

I've pre-loaded our existing data so you'll see familiar names.

A short user guide covering all the basics:
[link or path to USER_GUIDE.md]

If something looks wrong or you can't find something, just let me know.

[Your name]
```

## PSM-Specific Version (Slack / Teams)

For PSM users who only see their assigned partners:

```
Hey [Name] — the partner platform is live. Your login gives you access to
your assigned accounts: [Partner A], [Partner B], [Partner C].

🔗 URL: [your platform URL]

Username: [their username]
Temp password: [temp password] — change it on first login (top-right menu)

Your view shows:
• Your assigned partners — full profiles, contacts, match history
• Notes — add and view meeting notes for your accounts
• Requests — service requests assigned to you

Everything is scoped to your accounts. If you need access to a partner
that's not showing up, let me know and I'll update your assignments.
```

---

## Notes for Admins

- Create each user's account first (Admin → Users → Add User) before sending this
- For PSM users, complete their partner assignments (Admin → Partner Assignments)
  before they log in — otherwise they'll see an empty screen
- Set a temporary password you can share, then the user sets their own on first login
- If your platform is on a VPN or private network, include VPN instructions
