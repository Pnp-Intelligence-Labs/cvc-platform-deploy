Common causes

  • org_internal — OAuth consent screen is set to Internal. Change it to External in GCP Console, or add your email as a test user.
  • redirect_uri_mismatch — Add http://127.0.0.1:8085/oauth2callback to Authorized redirect URIs in your GCP OAuth client.
  • access_denied — You declined Drive access. Try again and click Allow.
