# Plug and Play Vertical OS — Vision

## The Problem

VC teams run their operations across too many disconnected tools — a CRM they don't own, spreadsheets that go stale, Slack threads that lose context, and external platforms that hold their data hostage.

When a team wants to understand their own pipeline, they're asking their data vendor's support team.

## The Solution

A self-hosted platform the team actually owns. One place for deal flow, partner relationships, service requests, and accountability. Data lives in their database, on their server.

## Design Principles

1. **Data ownership first.** Teams should never need a vendor to access their own data.
2. **Internal ops, not reporting.** The platform drives team workflow. Reporting to LPs or partners is a plugin.
3. **Configurable, not custom.** Teams configure the platform for their vertical — sectors, roles, partners, fund structure — without code changes.
4. **Plugins, not bloat.** Features that don't apply to every team ship as optional plugins. Core stays lean.
5. **Accountability by design.** Every action is attributed. Leaderboards, activity logs, and request workflows create a culture of ownership.

## The Flywheel

The platform gets more valuable the more the team uses it. Notes create briefing context. Requests create accountability. Pipeline activity creates pattern data. The team's institutional knowledge lives in the platform, not in people's heads.

## Long-Term

A marketplace of plugins — LP reporting, sector intelligence, DD workflows, meeting intelligence — that teams can add as they scale. Built and distributed by us, installed by them.
